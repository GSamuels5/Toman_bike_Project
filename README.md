# Bike Share Pricing Strategy Analysis

## Business Question
Can the company increase prices next year without negatively affecting demand?

## Dataset
Bike Share data (2021–2022)

## Tools Used
- SQL (CTE, Revenue & Profit Calculation)
- Power BI (Dashboard & KPI Modeling)

## Key Findings
- 25% price increase
- 64% demand increase
- Price elasticity: 2.56 (Elastic demand)

## Recommendation
A 10–15% price increase is recommended instead of 25% to avoid demand drop.
